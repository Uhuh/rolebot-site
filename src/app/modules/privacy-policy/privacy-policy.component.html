Privacy Policy

By using RoleBot's website and Discord bot and interacting with our service, you agree to our privacy policy. RoleBot does not and will never share your data with any third parties.

Data Collection and Use

RoleBot is a Discord bot that allows users to give and take roles based on the emoji they react to on messages. In order to provide this service, RoleBot collects and stores certain information, including guild ID's, role ID's, role names, emoji's and emoji ID's, message ID's, and channel ID's. This information is collected and stored solely for the purpose of providing the RoleBot service. It is not shared with any third parties, and users can only access their own guild data.

Data Removal

You have the right to request the removal of your data. You can request your data to be deleted by joining the Discord server and requesting there or by emailing dylan@panku.io with a subject line of RoleBot data removal and including your server's id.
